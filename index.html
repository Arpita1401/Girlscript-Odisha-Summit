About

Hey Geeks!

Girlscript Odisha is back with its Annual Regional Summit, with a huge amount of opportunities and exposure for all budding and curious technocrats. This week-long Regional summit parallelly brings you an exciting platform for your Tech and Non-tech skills.


Girlscript, one of the biggest nonprofit tech organizations. Girlscript aims at changing lives by imparting tech education to everyone. We want to create awareness about technology and want to impart knowledge to our budding technocrats and aspiring students. Girlscript is the largest and fastest-growing tech community in India. Girlscript’s mission is to encourage individuals to learn how to code and stand out among the crowd. It also supports girls and encourages them to take part and learn new technology. Girlscript helps you to learn many technologies and makes you an expert in specific fields.
